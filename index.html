How to Play

1
Choose Your Mode

Play solo against the AI or challenge a friend in Multiplayer mode on the same device.

2
Pick Your Symbol

Select X or O and choose a color theme for your side before starting.

3
Take Turns

Players alternate clicking any empty cell on the 3×3 grid to place their symbol.

4
Win the Game

Get 3 of your symbols in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win!

5
Play Again

Match history is tracked. Use the Pause menu to replay or head back to the main menu anytime.

About PlayXO

PlayXO is a beautifully designed Tic-Tac-Toe experience built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, just clean code and great design.

Features include a smart AI opponent, match history tracking, customizable themes, and a smooth animated interface designed for all screen sizes.
